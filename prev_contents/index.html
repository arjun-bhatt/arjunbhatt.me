Are you down?

Who's down is the premiere platform to connect event creators and event seekers. I'm realizing I don't actually have a huge amount of content to put here as I'm working towards a pretty tight deadline but throughout this course I will continue to work on this project and hopefully it'll work out.

For organizations, clubs, & individuals. Available on iOS, Android, and Web.

For event creators

  1. Be spontaneous! Post your event no more than 12 hours in advance.
  2. Choose tags to send notifications to users subscribed to those feeds.
  3. PUT IT ON THE MAP! and have a blast!

For event seekers

  1. You down to, like, do something?
  2. Open the map!
  3. Join an event, chat with the event creator, and meet up and do something!
    • Note: in the time of COVID-19, maybe don't meet up. But still do things!!!

Try it yourself!
